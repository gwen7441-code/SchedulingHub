# Boss Approval Checklist

Use this before production launch.

- Project scope is approved
- Instructor access rules are approved
- Administrator and super-admin access rules are approved
- Phone OTP cost and sender policy are approved
- SendGrid email cost and sender identity are approved
- Apple Developer account is available
- Google Play Console account is available
- Microsoft Dynamics integration is approved
- Privacy review is approved
- Course-attendee fields shown to instructors are approved
- Data retention policy is approved
- Push notification policy is approved
- Administrator notification recipients are approved
- Instructor availability policies are approved
- Assignment acceptance and decline policies are approved
- Launch date is approved
- Test group is selected
- Support owner is assigned
